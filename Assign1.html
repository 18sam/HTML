Antarctica

The continent of Antarctica makes up most of the Antarctic region. The Antarctic is a cold, remote area in the Southern Hemisphere encompassed by the Antarctic Convergence.

Antarctica is the fifth-largest continent in terms of total area. The Antarctic Ice Sheet dominates the region. It is the largest single piece of ice on Earth.

This ice sheet even extends beyond the continent when snow and ice are at their most extreme.

The most familiar animal of Antarctica is probably the penguin. Their feathers retain a layer of air, helping them keep warm in the freezing water.
